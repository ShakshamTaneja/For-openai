# VOIDLEX — AI Legal-Tech Infrastructure

VOIDLEX is an AI-assisted legal-tech and cybersecurity infrastructure project focused on intelligent legal workflows, digital governance systems, automation, and secure operational tooling.

The live public deployment is available at:

https://voidlex.imperialecc.com/

---

## About The Project

VOIDLEX is being developed as a next-generation legal and operational intelligence platform combining:

- AI-assisted legal workflows
- Intelligent document processing
- Legal research automation
- Cybersecurity-oriented infrastructure
- Secure digital governance systems
- Privacy-conscious architecture

The project is part of a broader ecosystem of educational, cybersecurity, and AI infrastructure initiatives developed under independent research and engineering efforts.
